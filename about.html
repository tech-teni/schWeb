Welcome to Our School

Precious Muslim Sprouts Schools started operation in January 2009 at No 5 Olabisi Olaitan street, Omolade, Igando, Lagos with two students in a private apartment. The Founder and Proprietor is Mrs Madeenah Gbadamosi

It was started to fulfill a need in giving the Muslim child quality education without compromising his or her religious beliefs. The school was raised on six pillars; Islam Science, Technology, Engineering, Art and Maths, as it’s core goals (I - STEAM)

Over the years, the school has grown to fulfill its objectives and maintain a place of reference amongst schools. The school moved from its room location to a flat and by the grace of Allah occupies its own building now. The student capacity has also grown from the humble 2 to over 200 students, many of whom have passed out to Federal Government Colleges, Model colleges, Paramilitary and Higher institutions and are making us proud.

The passion that drives this noble cause is the desire to see Muslim kids excel in any area they find themselves and instill in them the passion to serve Allah. We want them to have the best of this world and the hereafter.

Today Precious Sprouts school has grown to cater for the needs of the Muslim families in and around Alimosho Local Government Area, served as a growth haven for its teachers who have experienced high quality teaching and professional development while nurturing the students to excel in their academic and religious lives.
